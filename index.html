What can we do

To create a plastic-free environment, we must embrace a multi-faceted approach that encompasses reducing, reusing, and recycling. Firstly, reducing our reliance on single-use plastics is essential. This means opting for reusable alternatives like water bottles, bags, and containers. Secondly, we can encourage responsible consumption by supporting businesses that offer products with minimal packaging. Additionally, fostering awareness and education on the environmental impact of plastic pollution is crucial. Recycling plays a pivotal role in this journey; properly disposing of plastics ensures they don't end up in our oceans and landfills
